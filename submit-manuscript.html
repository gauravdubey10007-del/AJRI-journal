How It Works

Three ways to reach us

Submission form

Pre-submission enquiry

Unsure about fit? Email your abstract to editor@ajri.org for a quick scope check before preparing a full submission.

After You Submit

What happens next

Acknowledgement

You receive a manuscript ID within 48 hours.

Editorial screening

Scope, formatting, and plagiarism checks — typically within one week.

Double-blind peer review

At least two independent expert reviewers, typically 3–6 weeks.

Decision & publication

Accepted articles are copyedited and published online first, open access.
