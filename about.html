About the journal

The American Journal of Research & Innovation (AJRI) is an international, multidisciplinary, peer-reviewed, open-access journal committed to advancing knowledge across the sciences and beyond.

Our Mission

Advancing global research

AJRI exists to give researchers, healthcare professionals, educators, engineers, innovators, policymakers, and industry experts a rigorous, accessible platform for sharing scholarly work. We believe high-quality research should be freely available to everyone, everywhere — without paywalls or barriers.

Every submission is evaluated on scientific merit through a fair, transparent peer-review process, regardless of the author's geography, institution, or career stage.

Our Vision

Research without borders

To become a trusted international venue for multidisciplinary research and innovation — connecting scholars across continents, disciplines, and sectors, and translating knowledge into real-world impact.
